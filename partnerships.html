Strategic Partnerships

Building Cambridge's sports innovation ecosystem together

Collaboration at Our Core

Faster Future Sports is built on strategic partnerships that unite Cambridge's world-class research institutions, innovative startups, elite sports organizations, and industry leaders.

Our collaborative approach ensures that breakthrough research translates into real-world impact, creating value for athletes, teams, investors, and the broader sports industry.

The Cambridge Ecosystem We're Building Within

The institutions and assets we aim to build partnerships around

🎓 University of Cambridge

World-leading research departments in engineering, life sciences, computer science, and psychology — a natural home for sports innovation research.

🏛️ Anglia Ruskin University

Strong sports science, performance analysis, and athlete development programmes we hope to collaborate with.

⚽ Cambridge United FC

A potential real-world testing ground for performance technologies, youth development, and community engagement.

🏥 Cambridge Biomedical Campus

Europe's largest biomedical research hub — an exciting opportunity for sports medicine, regenerative therapies, and health innovation.

These institutions are part of the Cambridge ecosystem we are actively building relationships within. Partnerships are in development — get in touch to collaborate.
