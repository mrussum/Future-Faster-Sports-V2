Joseph Keady at Cambridge University

Founder & CEO

Joseph Keady

Pioneering the future of sports innovation from the heart of Cambridge.

Joseph Keady brings a unique blend of sports industry insight and entrepreneurial vision to Future Faster Sports. With deep roots in Cambridge's innovation ecosystem, Joseph recognized the untapped potential at the intersection of world-class academic research and the rapidly evolving sports industry.

His vision for FFS emerged from years of observing the gap between groundbreaking research emerging from Cambridge and other leading institutions, and the practical needs of the sports industry worldwide. Through FFS's nine-pillar framework, Joseph is building the infrastructure to bridge this gap systematically and sustainably.

"Cambridge has long been a powerhouse of innovation across technology, healthcare, and business," Joseph explains. "Yet sports innovation has remained fragmented. Future Faster Sports exists to create a comprehensive ecosystem where researchers, investors, athletes, and entrepreneurs can collaborate to solve real problems and create real value."
