Faster Future Sports Launches in Cambridge: Building the Premier Sports Innovation Ecosystem

Cambridge, UK - Today marks a significant milestone in the sports innovation landscape with the official launch of Faster Future Sports (FFS), a groundbreaking ecosystem designed to connect world-class research, cutting-edge technology, and sports organizations to accelerate athletic advancement.

"We're creating something truly unique," says Joseph Keady, Founder and CEO of Faster Future Sports. "Cambridge is already recognized as a global hub for innovation across numerous sectors. Now, we're bringing that same level of excellence and collaboration to the world of sports."
